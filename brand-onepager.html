Typography.
03 · Type System

Three families, three jobs. Fraunces for display. Inter for everything people actually read. JetBrains Mono for labels, coordinates, and numbers. No italic serif in body copy — that's a crutch we don't use.

Voice.
06 · Tone

Three principles that hold whether the copy is a button label, a lesson title, or a sales page.

01
Lethal Calm.
Short sentences. No hype. Authority comes from precision, not volume. The tone of a serious operator describing something he's done a thousand times.
02
Structural, not decorative.
Copy names the mechanism. If something is true, say why. If something is a claim, back it with a number, a story, or a rule. No adjectives doing the work a noun should be doing.
03
Masculine, not aggressive.
Direct, weighted, considered. The register of a man speaking to other men who are already serious. We don't sell by flexing — we speak like the work is already done.
Do / Don't.
07 · Guardrails
Do
  • Use Fraunces weight 400 with a terminal period for page and section titles.
  • Write kickers in sentence-case Inter with a period — they frame the title below.
  • Default body color to rgba(255,255,255,0.95). Only drop muted for explicitly secondary copy.
  • Use JetBrains Mono uppercase for labels, breadcrumbs, KPI counters.
  • Layer elevation with the defined surface tokens, not ad-hoc greys.
  • Maroon for signal — CTAs, active states, section markers.
  • Cream divider (#ECE5CB · 1px) for signature separators like under a masthead.
  • 150ms ease transitions — everywhere.
Don't
  • Use italic Fraunces mid-paragraph for editorial flourish.
  • Mix in Newsreader, Playfair, or any other serif.
  • Introduce bright reds (#E53E3E etc.) — the accent is maroon, not fire-engine red.
  • Use pure black #000000 for the base — the system is charcoal #1F1F1F.
  • Apply grunge textures, noise overlays, or cyberpunk glow effects.
  • Use flicker, pulse, glitch, or dash-flow animations — they belong to another brand.
  • Recolor, outline, stretch, or drop-shadow the logomark.
  • Let buttons or cards have inconsistent radii across a single page.
